# Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how RBX Labs handles information for the iPRD repository,
its GitHub Pages site, and associated deployment workflow.

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- Repository: `RBX-Labs/iPRD`
- GitHub Pages site: `https://rbx-labs.github.io/iPRD/`
- Deployment workflow integrations that publish iPRD HTML pages.

## Information We Process

We may process:
- HTML content you submit for deployment.
- Page metadata such as slug, title, and deployment timestamps.
- Technical and operational logs provided by GitHub and related infrastructure.

## Authentication Secrets

- Personal access tokens (PATs) are not stored by RBX Labs servers in this implementation.
- If entered in the browser UI, PATs are stored in browser localStorage on your device.
- You are responsible for securing your device, browser profile, and tokens.

## How We Use Information

We use submitted data to:
- Trigger GitHub Actions workflows.
- Publish requested iPRD pages.
- Maintain deployment records and debug operational issues.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by service providers required to
operate this system, including GitHub and hosting infrastructure.

## Retention

Published content and related metadata may be retained as long as required for operation,
audit, legal compliance, and business continuity.

## Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards. No method of transmission or
storage is 100% secure.

## Your Responsibilities

You must not submit sensitive personal data, regulated data, or confidential third-party
data unless you are explicitly authorized and compliant with applicable law.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date controls.

Privacy questions: legal@rbxlabs.io
